# Privacy Philosophy

We believe your data belongs to you. We make privacy-by-default applications for
seniors that revolve around that core idea. These are our core principles:

- We never sell data
- Users own all their data
- Encryption is on by default

These principle will never change, even if upholding them costs us revenue or
market share.

## Core Principles

1. **Privacy by Default**
   - We don't collect what we don't display
   - Users don't need to understand encryption to be private
   - Unused data is deleted after 30 days (for recovery requests)

2. **Data Minimization**
   - We do not collect your name, device IDs, or advertising IDS
   - Privacy is the feature that sets us apart. It isn't a feature, it's the
     core mission.

3. **User Ownership**
   - You own your data. Export it, take it to a competitor, delete it. It's
     yours.
   - When we structure data, we build with portability in mind. Where industry
     standards exist, we follow them to ensure you can take your data elsewhere.
     Where they don't exist, we create them.

4. **Transparency**
   - From time to time, we will provide updates to ensure your privacy. Those
     changes will be communicated via our privacy policy, found in this
     repository.
   - External audit reports will be provided as soon as they are available.

5. **Ecosystem Consistency**
   - We built this app from the ground up with privacy in mind, and created a
     secure foundation on which to base future privacy-first apps for seniors.

## This Philosophy Guides

- Feature prioritization: "Does this feature require collecting new data?"
- Hiring: We seek engineers who value privacy
- Partnerships: We reject integrations that violate user privacy
- Monetization: We will never sell user data

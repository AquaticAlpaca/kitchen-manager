# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-07-11 **Last Updated:** 2026-07-11

## Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. This app is built with
privacy-by-default: we collect only what we need to make the app work, we don't
sell your data, and you own everything you create.

---

## What We Collect

### Required for the App to Work

- **Your contact information** (email or phone)—for account access and password
  recovery
- **Your shopping lists, meal plans, and pantry inventory**—because you created
  this data; it's yours
- **Your family member sharing relationships**—so we know who can see shared
  lists
- **Your authentication method** (password, biometric preference)—to keep your
  account secure

### We Do NOT Collect

- Location data
- Contacts or address book
- Camera or microphone access
- Health data beyond what you enter
- Demographic information (age, income, health conditions)
- Advertising identifiers or tracking pixels
- Browsing history outside this app

---

## How We Use Your Data

| **Your Data**                             | **Why**                                      | **Who Sees It**                                         |
| ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| Shopping lists, meal plans                | To display and sync across your devices      | Only you (and family members you explicitly share with) |
| Email                                     | Authentication and account recovery          | Only our servers; we don't share                        |
| Sharing relationships                     | To know who can access shared lists          | Only the system; encrypted on transmission              |
| Usage patterns (e.g., "10 meals created") | To improve the app; anonymized after 90 days | Internal analytics only; never sold                     |

---

## Sharing With Family Members

When you share a list with someone:

- You can revoke access anytime; they lose access immediately
- Sharing links expire after 30 days of inactivity for security

---

## Data Storage & Security

- **Encryption in transit:** All data travels over HTTPS (industry-standard
  encryption)
- **Encryption at rest:** Your data is encrypted on our servers
- **Biometric lock:** Your phone requires Face ID or fingerprint to open the app
- **Local storage:** Shopping lists sync to your device's encrypted local
  database
- **Backup:** We keep encrypted backups for disaster recovery; deleted data is
  removed after 30 days

---

## Your Data Rights

### Access Your Data

You can export all your lists, recipes, and preferences as JSON anytime from app
settings.

### Delete Your Data

- Delete individual lists anytime—they're gone immediately
- Delete your entire account—all data purged within 30 days
- We don't retain backups of deleted accounts after 30 days

### Data Portability

You own your data. Export it, take it to a competitor, delete it. It's yours.

---

## Third-Party Services

We use **Supabase** to store and sync your data. Supabase is:

- Open-source (you can audit it)
- GDPR-compliant
- SOC 2 Type II certified

We **do not use:**

- Google Analytics or other tracking tools
- Facebook Pixel or advertising networks
- Third-party health data integrations (Apple Health, Fitbit, etc.) unless you
  enable them

---

## Children & Seniors

This app is designed for adults, particularly seniors and their caregivers. We
do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If a caregiver creates an
account for a senior, that senior owns the data in the account—the caregiver has
access only to shared content.

---

## Updates to This Policy

We may update this policy. If we make material changes (e.g., we start
collecting new data), we'll notify you via email and in the app. Continued use
means you accept the changes.
